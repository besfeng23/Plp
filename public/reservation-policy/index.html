Reservation Policy

Pueblo La Perla handles reservations through a reviewed request process. This keeps the stay private, avoids accidental double booking, and allows the team to prepare arrival, room, and concierge details properly.

Reservation process

  • Guests submit preferred accommodation, dates, guest count, contact details, and arrival notes.
  • The PLP team reviews availability and any special requests before final confirmation.
  • A reservation hold may be requested after a booking reference is created.
  • A stay is confirmed only after availability review, reservation completion, and confirmation from the team.

Availability and rates

Rates shown on the website are starting estimates and may vary by date, stay length, room readiness, guest requirements, and special arrangements.

Submitting a request does not automatically guarantee the room or dates. Final confirmation must come from Pueblo La Perla.

Cancellations and changes

Change and cancellation requests should be sent to the PLP team as early as possible. Refund handling, if applicable, is reviewed case by case based on reservation status, payment status, and timing.
