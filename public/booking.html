Reservation requests

Reservation requests are subject to availability. After you submit your preferred dates and accommodation, the PLP team will confirm availability, rate details, deposit requirements, and final booking terms.

Deposit and payment

A deposit may be required to secure selected dates. Payment timing, accepted methods, and remaining balance details will be confirmed before the reservation is finalized.

Cancellations and changes

Cancellation and date-change terms may vary by season, accommodation, and reservation arrangement. Guests should review the confirmed terms before payment.

Arrival details

Arrival coordination, transfer support, check-in guidance, and special requests can be arranged after the reservation inquiry is received.
