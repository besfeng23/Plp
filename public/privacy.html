Guest Privacy

Privacy

Pueblo La Perla collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries, review reservation requests, coordinate stays, and communicate with guests.

Information we may collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and contact details.
  • Preferred accommodation, dates, guest count, arrival notes, and special requests.
  • Booking references, reservation status, and payment verification status when a reservation is created.
  • Messages sent through email, forms, or concierge communication.

How information is used

Guest information is used to review availability, prepare reservation details, respond to inquiries, coordinate arrival, arrange requested services, and maintain booking records.

PLP does not sell guest information to advertisers.

Service providers

PLP may use trusted systems for website hosting, reservation records, email delivery, and online payment processing. These providers are used only to operate the booking and communication flow.

Retention

Reservation and inquiry records may be kept as needed for guest communication, operational records, accounting, dispute review, and service improvement.

Guest requests

Guests may request correction or deletion of personal information by emailing plpvillas@gmail.com. Some records may need to be retained where required for reservation, accounting, or dispute purposes.

For private or sensitive stay requests, guests may contact PLP directly by email instead of using the online form.
