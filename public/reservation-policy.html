Reservation Policy

Pueblo La Perla handles reservations through a reviewed request process. This keeps the stay private, avoids accidental double booking, and allows the team to prepare arrival, room, and concierge details properly.

Reservation process

1. Request

Guests submit preferred accommodation, dates, guest count, contact details, and arrival notes through the private reservation form.

2. Review

The PLP team reviews availability, guest details, and any special requests before issuing final confirmation.

3. Hold

After a booking reference is created, the next payment step is the 30% deposit. This supports the request while PLP reviews the preferred dates.

4. Confirmation

A stay is confirmed only after availability review, reservation completion, and confirmation from the PLP team.

Availability and rates

Rates shown on the website are starting estimates and may vary by date, stay length, room readiness, guest requirements, and special arrangements. Availability is checked before final confirmation.

Submitting a request does not automatically guarantee the room or dates. Final confirmation must come from Pueblo La Perla.

Reservation hold and payments

The 30% deposit step may be requested after the booking reference is created. Online processing, where used, happens only after the request step and is attached to the booking reference.

Payment verification may take time depending on the payment method or provider response. PLP issues final confirmation only after availability review and payment verification are complete.

Cancellations and changes

Change and cancellation requests should be sent to the PLP team as early as possible. Approval depends on the booking status, requested dates, room readiness, and any costs already committed for the stay.

Refund handling, if applicable, is reviewed case by case based on the reservation status, payment status, and timing of the request.

Direct assistance

Guests who prefer concierge assistance may email the team directly at plpvillas@gmail.com.
