Website Terms

Terms

These terms explain how guests should use the Pueblo La Perla website and how online reservation information should be understood.

Website information

Content on this website is provided for guest information, accommodation discovery, and reservation inquiry purposes. PLP may update accommodation descriptions, rates, availability, images, policies, and services at any time.

Rates and availability

Rates shown on the website are starting estimates unless confirmed directly by the PLP team. Availability shown or checked online is subject to final resort review.

A reservation is not final until PLP confirms the stay after availability and reservation review.

Reservation requests

Submitting a form creates a reservation request or booking reference. It does not automatically guarantee a room, date, rate, or special request.

Guest responsibility

  • Guests should provide accurate contact, guest count, arrival, and stay details.
  • Guests should review dates and room choice before submitting a request.
  • Guests should contact PLP promptly if details change.

Contact

Questions may be sent to plpvillas@gmail.com.
