Private Villas Boracay

Private hillside villas above Boracay.

Pueblo La Perla is designed for guests who want Boracay with more privacy, more space, and a quieter resort rhythm. The retreat sits above the island center and gives families, couples, and private groups a calmer base for villa living, wellness, dining, and curated island days.

Why choose a private villa stay?

Space

Private villa living gives guests room to gather, rest, dine, and move through the stay without the feeling of a crowded hotel corridor.

Discretion

The experience is quieter and more concierge-led, with room for family needs, arrival details, private dining, and wellness requests.

Signature stay: Grand Ocean Villa

The Grand Ocean Villa is the strongest private-villa option at PLP. It is positioned for up to 8 guests, with 4 bedrooms, private pool atmosphere, living space, kitchen comfort, and hillside views.

  • Best for families and private groups
  • Designed for longer stays and gatherings
  • Starting from ₱40,000 per night
  • Concierge can assist with transfers, meals, wellness, and sea days

Plan the stay around the guest

A private villa stay should not feel packaged. PLP can help shape the stay around arrival timing, wellness rhythm, family requirements, water activities, and quiet evenings after the island day.
