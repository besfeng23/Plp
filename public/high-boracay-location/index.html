High Boracay Location

A quieter hillside base near the island center.

Pueblo La Perla is positioned in High Boracay, giving guests a more private hillside retreat while remaining close enough to the island center for dining, beach days, water activities, and arrival coordination.

Why High Boracay matters

Privacy

The hillside setting gives guests more distance from the busiest island rhythm, especially useful for families, private groups, and wellness-focused stays.

Access

Guests can still plan beach days, dining, activities, and transfers with the support of the PLP team.

Arrival support

Guests may arrive through Caticlan or Kalibo. The team can help coordinate port timing, pickup support, and the final approach to Pueblo La Perla before arrival.

  • Caticlan arrival guidance
  • Kalibo arrival guidance
  • Port timing support
  • Transfer coordination

Stay types that fit the location

The location supports private villa stays, family trips, wellness-focused escapes, and longer stays where quiet mornings and restful evenings matter.
