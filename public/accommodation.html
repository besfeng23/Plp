Accommodation

Private stays above Boracay.

Choose between the resort’s signature villa, elevated suite, or quiet premium room. Each stay is positioned for privacy, comfort, and direct concierge coordination.

How to choose

The Grand Ocean Villa is the strongest choice for families and private groups. The Sunset Suite suits quiet couples or small families. The Smart Room Premium is ideal for shorter stays and guests who want a calm modern base.

Room Guidance

Choose by the stay you want.

For privacy and space

Choose Grand Ocean Villa for the strongest private-stay feeling, more room to gather, and the most complete villa experience.

For quiet island ease

Choose Sunset Suite for a softer, lighter stay with more privacy than a standard hotel room and less scale than a villa.

For short premium stays

Choose Smart Room Premium for a calm modern base when the trip is shorter, lighter, or more work-friendly.
