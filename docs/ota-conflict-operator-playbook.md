# OTA Conflict Operator Playbook

## Critical direct booking overlap

Maintain the direct booking as protected. Review the OTA/staged item and choose contact OTA, reject OTA import, or manual entry only after staff approval.

## Manual block overlap

Maintain the manual block unless ownership releases inventory. Do not override a manual block automatically.

## Duplicate import

Find the original staged import and archive the duplicate after confirming it is the same OTA reservation reference.

## Missing mapping

Update room or rate mapping before approving manual entry.

## Payment ambiguity

Review OTA payment terms. Do not change PLP payment status from the conflict console.

## Channel disabled or credentials missing

Keep disabled. Configure credentials outside the repository only after an approved operational process.

## Escalation

Escalate critical or high conflicts to operations leadership before changing guest-facing plans.
